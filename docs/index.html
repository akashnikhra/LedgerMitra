Features

[+] Invoicing — Create GST-ready sales invoices with line items, auto-numbering, and CGST/SGST/IGST support.
[+] Dashboard — KPI cards, monthly revenue trends, top debtors, invoice aging, and low stock alerts — all at a glance.
[+] Purchase Invoices — Record purchases with stock updates and ledger integration. Full GST support.
[+] Receipts — Receive payments with auto-allocation to oldest outstanding invoices or manual per-invoice assignment.
[+] Ledger — Customer-wise and year-wise ledger with debit/credit columns, running balance, and date range filters.
[+] Products & Stock — SKU-based product catalog with rates, GST, HSN codes, stock tracking, and reorder alerts.
[+] Customers & Suppliers — Party master management with opening balances, GSTIN, state, and full transaction history.
[+] Legacy Import — Full migration engine from Speed Plus MDB/BMW files. Multi-FY detection, deduplication, audit logging.
[*] Private & Offline — Your financial data never leaves your machine. Single SQLite file. Backup by copying it.
[+] WhatsApp Integration — Send invoice and receipt PDFs directly via WhatsApp Web with customizable message templates.
[+] Print & PDF — Invoice, receipt, and ledger printing with save-to-PDF.
[+] Backup & Restore — One-click database export and import.

Why LedgerMitra?

Modern accounting without the cloud — built for Windows, built for privacy.

Offline-First

All data lives in a local SQLite database. No server, no cloud dependency, no monthly fees. Backup is a file copy.

Double-Entry Ledger

Every invoice, receipt, and payment creates debit/credit entries. Balances are always consistent — no reconciliation surprises.

GST-Ready

CGST + SGST (intra-state) and IGST (inter-state) with configurable rates per product. Auto-calculated on every invoice.

Invoice Aging

Outstanding amounts tracked per invoice with aging buckets for follow-up. Know exactly who owes what and since when.

Balance Carry-Forward

Customer opening balances flow across financial years with full audit trail via fy_carry_forwards.

Stock Tracking

Product stock updated on invoice/purchase creation. Reorder level alerts on dashboard — never run out of tracked items.

Auto-Numbering

Invoice and receipt numbers auto-generated per financial year. No gaps, no duplicates, no manual entry.

Payment Allocation

Receipts can be auto-allocated against oldest outstanding invoices or assigned manually — flexible to your workflow.

Migration from Speed Plus

LedgerMitra provides a complete migration pipeline from Speed Plus (.mdb / .bmw) files. Your data, your history — moved in one pass.

[+] Items → Products (SKU, rates, GST, stock)
[+] Account → Customers (party ledgers, opening balance)
[+] BillMaster → Invoices (sales vouchers)
[+] Ledger → Ledger Entries (payments / receipts)
[+] Company → Company (name during wizard)
[+] Multi-FY detection within a single MDB file
[+] Deduplication via file hash tracking
[+] Balance carry-forward across financial years
[+] Audit logging with skipped item details
